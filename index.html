Jollof Rice
Smoky tomato rice dish central to Nigerian Parties
Ingredients
  • 500g Long grain rice
  • 3 spoons Margarine/Vegetable oil
  • 2 Large Onions
  • 6+ Red chilli peppers
  • 3 Fresh tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons Tomato purée
  • 1 clove Garlic/ginger
  • Bay leaves
  • Thyme
  • 3 Maggi cubes
  • 500g Chicken
  • 100g Boiled seasoned snails
  • 40g Cooked green peas
  • 2 Smoked stock fish (optional)
  • 4 Crabs or 50g Shrimps
  • Plantains
  • 500ml water
Instructions
  1. Heat butter/oil, fry onions, garlic, tomato purée, chillis, ginger, seasoning.
  2. Add rice, bay leaf.
  3. Boil shrimps, crab, snails, season, add peas.
  4. Season chicken, grill with onions, garlic, ginger.
  5. Add shrimps, peas to rice to garnish.
  6. Make sauce with oil, onions, pepper, tomato purée, snail, crab, stock fish, chicken and seasoning.
  7. Stir cooked rice, garnish and serve with plantain and sauce.
